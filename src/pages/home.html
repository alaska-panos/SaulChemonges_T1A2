My summary skill set

Healthy animals translates to having healthy people and lifestyles.

Apart from being a seasoned veterinary surgeon, I am skilled in IT-intensive discovery mass spectrometry-based proteomics on the TripleTOF 5600 (SCIEX) instrument, analysis of large-scale proteome-wide datasets, proteogenomic method development; including peptide spectral library (PSL) construction using data-depended acquisition (DDA) workflows and SWATH-MS proteomic assays.

I am organised in approach to work tasks, proficient in using common Microsoft® Office computer applications, EndNote reference management package and bioinformatics platforms specifically SWATH pipeline (SCIEX), ProteinPilot™, Mascot, Scaffold, Skyline, PeptideShaker, PeakView® and MarkerView™ applications.

I have advanced proficiency in raw data computer inputting, organising and processing experimental or clinical research information in spreadsheets. I am competent in using Laboratory Information Management Systems (LIMS) and eager to continue learning, developing and using more computer-based applications to enhance my prior attributes of writing scientific conference papers and peer-review works.
